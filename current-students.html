Tony Wise


Tony Wise of Weatherford completed his first solo flight on December 16, 2011 at the Thomas P. Stafford Airport. 

 
 

According to aviation tradition, Wise had to lose his shirt tail to his instructor, Gregg Spencer, to celebrate his successful solo. Wise was happy to give up his shirt tail in exchange for the privilege to solo, “This shirt tail is going on my wall!” Wise beamed.
