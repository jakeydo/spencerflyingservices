Note from Gregg

I cannot remember a time in my life that I have not been totally obsessed with airplanes and flying. As a young child, it was always thrilling whenever an airplane flew over and no matter what I was doing, I had to stop and look up to watch the aircraft fly out of sight. In fact, even today I can't resist the urge to stop and look whenever an airplane flies over.

Since then, I have had many planes, aircraft ratings and flying experiences. Since my first Cessna 152 back in 1992, to my  Piper Comanche I bought in 2009, I have loved it all. Then in 2011, I began Spencer Flying Services and am having a wonderful time with clients of all kinds. I love every each flight and look forward to many more.

Building an Aircraft

One of Gregg's favorite hobbies is building his very own aircraft. He is currently in the process of building his very on Van's RV-7: a side-by-side two seat kit aircraft. You can click on the picture to the left to follow his progress on his very informative blog where details and documents the entire process. So far, he has spent about 400 hours on it, with many more to go!
